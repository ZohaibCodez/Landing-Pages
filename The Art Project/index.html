IS CALLED ART?

Art, also called (to distinguish it from other art forms) visual art, a visual object or experience consiously created through an expression of skill or imagination.

7 TYPES OF ART

The seven different types of art forms are: Painting, Sculpture, Architecture, Music, Literature, Film, and Theater.. However,back in the olden days, the seven different art forms were called the liberal arts,consisting of grammar, rhetoric, logic, arithmetic, geometry, music, and astronomy.
